# **App Name**: PeterSmart Link

## Core Features:

- Homepage Display: Display a modern, clean homepage with a hero section, business introduction, highlighted services, and calls-to-action (e.g., 'Contact Us', 'Get Service', 'Learn More').
- About Us Page: Dedicated page detailing the founder's story (Peter Katabira), business mission ('To provide reliable and affordable tech solutions to the community'), and history (founded 2023).
- Services Showcase: A comprehensive page detailing all services offered, categorized with descriptions and relevant icons, including Computer Services, Website Development, App Development, Electronics & Accessories, Media Center, Digital Library, and Software Updates.
- Blog Management System: A system for creating, publishing, and managing blog posts with categories (Tech Tips, Software Guides, Business Updates, Tutorials) to provide valuable content and improve SEO.
- Contact and Inquiry System: A dedicated contact page featuring an inquiry form, email (support@petersmartlink.com), phone (+256756306040), and a direct WhatsApp chat button (+256775912582).
- Testimonials Display: Section on the website to showcase customer testimonials, providing social proof for the business's services (initially with placeholder content).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary colors: Blue, Black, and White to reflect a professional, youthful, and tech-inspired aesthetic.
- Modern sans-serif fonts like 'Inter' for high legibility and a contemporary digital feel.
- Utilize clean, modern icons relevant to technology, business services, and user interaction, complementing the overall tech-focused design.
- Clean and organized layout with ample whitespace, ensuring mobile responsiveness across all devices. Employ a clear, intuitive navigation menu.
- Subtle and swift animations for interactive elements and page transitions, enhancing user engagement without distraction.
